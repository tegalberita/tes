##### The ingredients needed to make Simple pilau:

1. Get 3 potatoes
1. Get Half kg of meat (boiled)
1. Make ready 1 sachet tomato paste
1. Prepare  Pilau masala and any other spice of your choice
1. Prepare  Onion,garlic and ginger
