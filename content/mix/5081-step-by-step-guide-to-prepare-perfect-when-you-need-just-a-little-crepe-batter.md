##### The ingredients needed to make When You Need Just a Little Crepe Batter:

1. Get 30 grams Egg
1. Take 1 tbsp Sugar
1. Prepare 50 ml Milk
1. Prepare 20 grams Cake flour
1. Get 5 grams Vegetable oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make When You Need Just a Little Crepe Batter:

1. Add the sugar to the egg and beat together well. Microwave for 30 seconds (without wrap) and dissolve the sugar. Sift the flour into the milk.
1. Mix the flour and milk until it is no longer floury, and then mix in the egg and sugar mixture from Step 1. Then mix in the vegetable oil.
1. Grease a medium-sized frying pan with a thin layer of oil (not listed in ingredients) and pour in the crepe batter. Set the heat to low-medium and cook the batter until the surface is no longer liquid.
1. If you add a splash of vanilla oil (essence) or rum to the batter you&#39;ll get even more flavour.
