##### The ingredients needed to make Kabocha Squash Pie with Steamed Kabocha in a Silicone Steamer:

1. Make ready 1 sheet Frozen puff pastry
1. Make ready 1 Plain flour (cake flour)
1. Take 1/2 Egg yolk
1. Take 1 Parchment paper
1. Get  Kabocha squash filling:
1. Get 100 grams Kabocha squash
1. Get 2 tsp Water
1. Get 10 grams Butter (or margarine)
1. Get 1 and 1/2 tablespoons Sugar
1. Get 2 tsp Milk




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Kabocha Squash Pie with Steamed Kabocha in a Silicone Steamer:

1. Take the puff pastry out of the freezer. Thaw at room temperature until softened.
1. Peel the kabocha squash and cut into 1-cm cubes. Place it in a Lekue steamer case and add some water.
1. Cover the case with the lid and microwave it at 600 W for about 3 minutes. Let it sit for 2 minutes to allow it to steam.
1. Add the butter to the kabocha while it is still hot. Use a potato masher to mash the kabocha squash. Add the sugar and milk and mix well.
1. Dust your working surface with cake flour. Roll out the puff pastry with rolling pin until it doubles in size.
1. Cut the puff pastry into 4 equal sections. Place 1/4 of the filling from Step 4 onto each section.  Brush on the beaten egg yolk onto the bottom half of the puff pastry. (Reserve a small amount of egg yolk.)
1. Fold the top half of puff pastry onto the bottom half. Using a fork, press to seal the 3 edges.
1. Pierce some holes in the top puff pastry from Step 7 with a fork.
1. Place the dough from Step 8 onto a parchment-paper-lined cookie sheet. Brush the reserved egg yolk on top for glazing.
1. Bake the dough from Step 9 in a preheated oven at 200℃ for about 12 minutes or until the pies puff up and the tops become golden.
