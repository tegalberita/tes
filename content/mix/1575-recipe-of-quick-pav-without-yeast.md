##### The ingredients needed to make Pav without yeast:

1. Make ready 2 cups refined flour
1. Get 2 tsp powdered sugar
1. Prepare Pinch salt
1. Prepare 3 tbsp curd
1. Prepare 3 tbsp oil
1. Get 1 tsp baking powder
1. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
1. Get 1/4 cup milk
1. Get 1/4 tsp white til




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Pav without yeast:

1. Sieve refined flour, powdered sugar, salt, baking powder and baking soda in a bowl. Now mix curd and oil to it. Make a soft dough with the help of milk. Knead it well with the help of oil.
1. Now make small balls from this dough. Knead these balls by folding towards inner side. Put some tills on the top.Brush it with milk.
1. Preheat the oven at 180 degree at convection mode. Grease the baking tray with oil.keep these balls in the oven and bake it around 15- 20 min. After removing from oven brush it with oil or butter. Now cover these pavs with a wet cloth for half an hour. Spongy pav is ready to serve.
