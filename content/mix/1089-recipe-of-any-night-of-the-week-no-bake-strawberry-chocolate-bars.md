##### The ingredients needed to make No bake strawberry chocolate bars:

1. Prepare 32 oreo cookies
1. Get 100 grams butter
1. Prepare 200 ml heavy cream
1. Prepare 250 grams dark chocolate
1. Take 1 cup fresh strawberries




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make No bake strawberry chocolate bars:

1. Finely crush the oreo cookies in a food processor or a grinder.
1. Pour melted butter over the crushed cookies and blend again until well combined.
1. Take out the cookie mixture in 8×8 inch pie mould and evenly spread with back of the spoon.
1. Refrigerate untill you prepare the filling.
1. Take heavy cream in a small sauce pan and place it over medium-low heat for few minutes. The cream just need to be hot, do not boil it.
1. Pour the cream over chocolate pieces, let it rest for 1-2 minutes.
1. Mix well with the help of a spatula until you get a smooth consistency.
1. Pour chocolate filling over the oreo crust and top with fresh strawberries.
1. Refrigerate for 1 hour. Once done cut in pieces and serve!
