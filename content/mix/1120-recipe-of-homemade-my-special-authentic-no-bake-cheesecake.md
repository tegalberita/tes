##### The ingredients needed to make My Special Authentic No-Bake Cheesecake:

1. Prepare  The base:
1. Prepare 100 grams Biscuits
1. Prepare 50 grams Unsalted butter
1. Take  *No-bake Cheesecake Batter*
1. Make ready 200 grams Cream cheese
1. Get 70 grams Sugar
1. Get 50 grams Yogurt
1. Prepare 50 ml Milk
1. Take 1 tbsp Lemon juice
1. Make ready 5 grams Gelatin
1. Make ready 30 ml Water
1. Make ready 200 ml Heavy cream




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make My Special Authentic No-Bake Cheesecake:

1. Crumble the biscuits into approximate sizes, blend in a food processor, add to a bowl, place together with the melted butter, and mix until the batter becomes moistened.
1. Pack Step 1 into a mold using the back of a spoon, and chill in the fridge.
1. Whip the heavy cream in another bowl until soft peaks form.
1. Combine the water and gelatin, and dissolve in the microwave or in a hot water bath.
1. Add the cream cheese in after returning to room temperature, add in the sugar, mix with an egg beater, add in the yogurt and milk, and mix some more.
1. Add in the lemon juice and gelatin, and mix well.
1. Add Step 6 to the bowl from Step 3, and stir well until it all mixes together.
1. Pour Step 7 into the mold that was stored in the fridge, smooth out the surface, and chill in the fridge for 2~3 hours.
1. It&#39;s done.
