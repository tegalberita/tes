##### Instructions to make Noodles Spring Roll:

1. In a bowl, take the flour and add baking soda, salt and water to make a semi-thick batter. Give it a rest for 8-10 minutes then make the pudla from it. Arrange the pudla with a layer of flour one on top of the other. Now spring roll sheet is ready. Now you can make a sheet of any shape and from this method. You can make many stuffed dishes like spring roll, samosa, ravioli using this sheet.
1. In a pan, saute onion and garlic in oil to make stuffing. Then add finely chopped capsicum, salt and noodles seasoning. Then add noodles and mix well. The noodles stuffing is ready.
1. Now take a sheet, put stuffing in it, roll it and seal the side with remaining mainda flour batter. In this way, make all the spring rolls and shallow fry them on low heat. Noodles spring roll is ready.
1. To make a tangy dip, take sezwan sauce and tomato catch-up in a bowl and mix it well. Tangy Dip is ready to accompany our noodles spring roll.
