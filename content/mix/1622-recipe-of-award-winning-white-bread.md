##### The ingredients needed to make White bread:

1. Get 400 g flour
1. Make ready 5 ml 1 tspn yeast
1. Take  Warm water
1. Get 3 tbspn sugar
1. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
1. Get  Nutmeg
1. Prepare 1/8 tspn bicarbonate
1. Take 2 tbspn milk
1. Get 1 pinch Cinnamon powder
1. Prepare 1 egg




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make White bread:

1. In a bowl soak yeast and sugar with warm water and let it sit 4 5mins
1. Mix all ur dry ingredients together and add it d soaked yeast to d flour add egg and knead d dough well with ur butter on d working surface and mould it
1. Brush it with butter and allow it to raise for 30-45minutes in a room temperature or under d sun but cover it
1. Bake for 30-40 at 180°
