1. Get 1/2 tsp Baking soda
1. Prepare 1/4 tsp Salt
1. Prepare 1/2 cup (or 1 stick) softened butter
1. Make ready 1 1/4 cup Sugar
1. Take 1 Egg
1. Take 1 tsp Vanilla
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Sour cream
