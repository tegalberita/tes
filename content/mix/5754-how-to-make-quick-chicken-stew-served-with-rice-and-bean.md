##### The ingredients needed to make Chicken Stew Served with Rice and Bean:

1. Take 2 cups rice
1. Take 1 cup beans
1. Get 15 fresh tomatoes
1. Get 5 tatashe
1. Make ready 5 fresh pepper
1. Get 1 large onion
1. Prepare 2 garlic clove
1. Take 1 kg chicken
1. Get 2 seasoning
1. Get 1 tsp curry powder
1. Prepare  fried plantain to serve
1. Prepare 1/2 cup vegetable oil
1. Make ready to taste salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Chicken Stew Served with Rice and Bean:

1. Wash beans and rice separately and parboil in different pot and remove from heat once they are soft and tender; remember to wash rice twice during this process before cooking the final time
1. Wash chicken and spice to your taste and steam till the beef is tender then set aside
1. Wash tomatoes, pepper, onions, garlic and tatashe and blend everything together and set aside
1. Heat up pot and add veg oil then fry the chicken briefly then add the tomatoes paste that was blended stir and after 15minutes of frying and stir occasionally
1. Add seasoning, curry powder and salt lower heat and let it cook for another 5 minutes turn off heat
1. Serve chicken stew with rice and beans while its still hot from the pot
