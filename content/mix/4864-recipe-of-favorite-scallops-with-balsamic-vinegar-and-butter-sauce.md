##### The ingredients needed to make Scallops with Balsamic Vinegar and Butter Sauce:

1. Prepare 10 to 15 Scallops (sashimi grade)
1. Take 1 Salt and pepper
1. Get 1 tbsp Olive oil
1. Prepare 10 grams ◎ Butter
1. Prepare 2 tbsp ◎ Red wine
1. Make ready 1/2 tbsp ◎ Sugar
1. Get 2 tbsp ◎ Balsamic vinegar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Scallops with Balsamic Vinegar and Butter Sauce:

1. Pat dry the scallops. Season on both sides with salt and pepper, and saute in a heated pan with olive oil.
1. When both sides are golden brown, transfer to serving plates. Garnish with radish sprouts or cherry tomatoes on the side.
1. Use the pan with the scallop juices. Add the ◎ ingredients, and heat the pan to evaporate the alcohol in the wine. Pour the sauce over the scallops and it&#39;s done.
