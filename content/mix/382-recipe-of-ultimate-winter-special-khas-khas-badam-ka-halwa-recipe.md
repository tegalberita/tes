##### The ingredients needed to make Winter Special Khas Khas Badam Ka Halwa Recipe:

1. Get 1/2 tsp Elaichi(cardamom) powder

##### Instructions to make Winter Special Khas Khas Badam Ka Halwa Recipe:

1. After soking

1. Heat the pan
