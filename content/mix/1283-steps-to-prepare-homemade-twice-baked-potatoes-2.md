##### Steps to make Twice Baked Potatoes #2:

1. Pierce each potato several times with a small paring knife. Toss in olive oil. Season with dried spices and thyme. Wrap in foil. Bake at 375째F for approximately 1 hour or until potatoes are easily pierced with a paring knife.
1. When potatoes cool, scoop out pulp into a small mixing bowl. Add dill butter, cream, cheddar, salt, and black pepper. Whip until smooth with a mixer or smash with a potato smasher.
1. Stuff each potato skin with mashed potato mixture. Bake at 375째F for approximately 10-15 minutes or until mashed potato mixture is thoroughly heated.
