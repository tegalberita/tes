##### The ingredients needed to make Potato Balls:

1. Take 2 Potatoes
1. Take 2 tsp. Salt
1. Take 3 tsp. Pepper
1. Make ready 5 tbsp. melted butter
1. Make ready 1/4 cup milk (or until soft but not too stiff)
1. Take 1 egg
1. Make ready  Breadcrumbs




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Potato Balls:

1. Wash and Clean potatoes and put in boiling water for 25 minutes.
1. Test softness with fork, if the fork goes through easily but it didn&#39;t break, it&#39;s done.
1. Cool the potatoes so it wouldn&#39;t be too hot to handle
1. Mash the potatoes with fork or potato masher, milk, butter, salt and pepper. Mash together
1. Shape the mashed potato into circles using table spoons.
1. Roll in egg.
1. Cover with breadcrumbs and put into plate.
1. Freeze it for 30 minutes to one hour before frying.
1. Fry until golden brown and serve.
